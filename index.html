The IV International Pharmaceutical Forum of Uzbekistan

The flagship event of the year for pharmaceutical industry professionals in Central Asia, bringing together top leaders, experts, and market participants to discuss innovative solutions and drive industry development.

International Pharmaceutical Forum of Uzbekistan

The IV International Pharmaceutical Forum of Uzbekistan upholds its reputation as the leading international platform for key decision-makers in the pharmaceutical industry. This prestigious event will bring together top executives from major manufacturers and distributors, government and regulatory authorities, technology licensors, legal experts, and industry consultants, creating an unparalleled environment for strategic discussions, networking, and business growth.
